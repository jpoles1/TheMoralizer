How does The Moralizer work?

Submit your dilemna:

Every moralizer entry begins with a question: what is the right thing to do? The question can be about anything, from a real-life pickle to a theoretical ethical quandry. Write up some possible responses to your dilemna, and let the community decide what's right and wrong.

Pass Judgement:

Everyone has their ethical dilemnas, now you get to pass judgement! Vote on other's dilemnas, and every time you vote with the majority, you'll get a Logos to add to the pile of righteous loot displayed on your user profile. For every person that votes on your dilema, you'll earn some Ethos!

Moral Community:

Here at The Moralizer, we wish to cultivate a community of respect and morality! Please be nice to your fellow moralizer members, and report those who aren't being respectful. To keep things civil, please refrain from using the names of real life individuals!
